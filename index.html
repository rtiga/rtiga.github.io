Privacy Policy for eBSPS 2024 App

1. Introduction

Welcome to the eBSPS 2024 app, developed by Direktorat Rumah Swadaya, Kementerian Pekerjaan Umum dan Perumahan Rakyat. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information from you:

a. Sensitive Data:

  • Photo Gallery: We may access your photo gallery to allow you to upload photos for use within the app.
  • Location: We may collect information about your location to provide location-based services.
  • User ID: We may collect and store a unique identifier for your user account.
  • Camera: We may access your device's camera to enable you to take and upload photos directly within the app.

b. Other Data:

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including the type of device, operating system, and other diagnostic data.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use our app, including the features you use and the time you spend on the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service: We use your information to deliver the features and functionality of the app.
  • To improve our app: We use the information to understand how users interact with our app, allowing us to enhance the user experience.
  • To communicate with you: We may use your information to send you updates, notifications, and other information related to the app.
  • To ensure security: We use the information to protect the security and integrity of our app and your data.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • For legal reasons: We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or prevent fraud.

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to your personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.

  • Objection: You can object to the processing of your personal information under certain circumstances.
